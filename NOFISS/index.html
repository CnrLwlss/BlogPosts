NoFi Night at the S&S

21:52 Wednesday 14th May 2008
There was a bloody amazing No-Fi evening at the Star & Shadow last night. Cath & Phil Tyler started off with a gorgeous harmonic vocally folk set in the cinema, followed by some electronical and visual treats from Blevin Blectum and then some fupping hardcore shit from Monotonix. Those guys sure know how to party. I could literally feel my cerebrospinal fluid vibrating throughout the set. I can't remember ever hearing such a diverse and wondrous selection of music in one night before.

I totally recommend that you go and listen to stuff from all of these fine people.
