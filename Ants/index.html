Ants

16:57 Sunday 5th August 2007
Over the past few days, I have noticed that the ants living in the front wall of my house have been going a bit weird. Some of them have grown absolutely huge, and have grown wings. And they crawl slowly up the wall.







I hope they're not planning to invade my house. They will get their little ant asses whupped if they try anything cute. It's a bit alarming how many of them there are sometimes. Especially if it's sunny. They seem to like the sun.

They are a good excuse to try out my new macro extension rings. Pretty zoomy.

I have put piles of chilli powder at strategic locations around my house. Hopefully that will discourage them.
